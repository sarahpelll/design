What is good design?

Good design is a cohesive collection of ideas represented in varying ways. Good design blends all relevant visual aspects together to present information that previously had lesser outlets. On a lower level, good design also can be characterized by its aesthetics, originality, understandability, and effectiveness. In a constant strive for perfection, good design is only limited by one's own effort, or lackthereof.
